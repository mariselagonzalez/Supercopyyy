San Felipe BC

San Felipe is a town on the bay of San Felipe in the Gulf of California, Sea of Cortez in the Mexican state of Baja California, 190 km south of the United States border and within the municipality of Mexicali. The beach is one of the highlights in San Felipe. The sea is warm and calm, making San Felipe a hidden Baja paradise. There are many fun things to do in San Felipe like fishing, surfing, along with the entertainments of towable rides over San felipe's beach. Before San Felipe became popular as a tourist destination it was known for fishing.
